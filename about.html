About Trivia

Our Story

Trivia is a women's western-wear brand that celebrates the modern, confident woman. Founded with a vision to offer young, fashion-conscious women looks for every season and styles for every reason, we have grown to become one of the most trusted names in women's fashion.

Our journey began with a simple belief: every woman deserves to feel beautiful, confident, and comfortable in what she wears. Today, we engage with millions of women across 900+ retail touchpoints, including exclusive brand outlets, large format stores, and leading online fashion portals.

Our Mission

At Trivia, we believe fashion should be accessible, sustainable, and empowering. Our mission is to create clothing that makes every woman feel like the best version of herself, regardless of her age, body type, or personal style.

We are committed to using quality materials, ethical manufacturing practices, and inclusive sizing to ensure that every piece we create meets the highest standards of excellence.

Quality & Craftsmanship

Every Trivia piece is crafted with meticulous attention to detail. From fabric selection to the final stitch, our team of skilled artisans ensures that each garment meets our exacting standards.

We source the finest materials from around the world and work closely with our manufacturing partners to maintain the quality that our customers have come to expect and love.

Our Values

Customer First

Your satisfaction is our top priority. We're dedicated to providing exceptional service and quality products.

Sustainability

We're committed to reducing our environmental impact through sustainable practices and materials.

Quality

Every piece is crafted with premium materials and attention to detail for lasting beauty.

Inclusivity

Fashion is for everyone. We design for all body types and celebrate diversity.
